Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Retro Media Client is a personal media browsing and playback application designed to connect to a user-controlled, local or self-hosted media server.

Information We Collect

Retro Media Client does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information.

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs

Local Network Usage

The application communicates only with a media server specified by the user, typically on the same local network. Media metadata and streams remain within the user’s environment.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties under any circumstances.

Children’s Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the application’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
